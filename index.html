Our Menu

Every dish at S & T Foods has it's own story from the traditional recipes direct from Srilanka, to modern updates that tell the history of our team, of our cooks and chefs who have left their mark on our kitchen, and our serving staff and managers who have been ambassadors of hospitality. Our menu is never done - we find inspiration all around, in new ingredients, new approaches, and fresh takes on old standards.

Who are we

Our home located in Colombo District, where we invite guests to celebrate life and share sundry blends of modern and traditional Srilankan cuisine. Our goal at S & T Foods is to unify our guest’s cultures and spirits with our own, expressing passion and generosity in the cuisine we share. Our attentive staff ensures that your entire dining experience at S & T Foods is stellar from beginning to end.
